ABOUT
apple.com is the official website for Apple Inc., a multinational technology company known for its consumer electronics, software, and services.
The site provides information about Apple's products, including iPhones, iPads, Macs, Apple Watches, and more, as well as its software like iOS, macOS, and various services like iCloud and Apple Music.
It also features details about Apple's values, accessibility features, and environmental initiatives.
